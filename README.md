## Project Overview

This project analyzes a bank marketing campaign dataset containing customer demographic information, financial characteristics, and campaign interaction records.

The objective is to identify customer segments with the highest likelihood of subscribing to term deposit products and provide actionable recommendations for improving marketing performance.

## Key Insights

* Cellular communication generated the highest conversion rates.
* Customers aged 30–50 represented the most responsive segment.
* Management professionals and technicians demonstrated strong conversion performance.
* Customers without housing loans were more likely to subscribe.
* Mid-year campaigns generated the highest engagement.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize cellular communication channels.
2. Improve customer segmentation.
3. Target high-converting occupations.
4. Optimize campaign timing.
5. Implement personalized marketing strategies.
